Milk Silo

We are Manufacture & Supply of Milk Silo. It consists of a round stainless steel inner cylinder and a stainless steel outer shell. The middle layer is filled with polyester foam as a thermal insulation layer, which is conducive to heat preservation and storage.

SM ENGINEERING / Products

Milk Storage Tank

We are Manufacture & Supply of Milk Storage Tanks. Milk tanks are insulated storage tanks for unprocessed raw milk. This is generally stored in large vertical tanks. By means of side entry agitators, raw milk is kept in motion in order to prevent cream from forming. An angled bottom ensures good outflow.

Cold Room

We are install & Supply of Milk Storage Tanks.The best possible storage conditions are below 10 ° C. Thus, the activities of bacteria in milk are reduced to a minimum. It is also of great importance that the milk starts to be processed within 24 hours at the latest after milking. Milk, whose temperature is lowered to 10 ° C, gets harder to process as time passes.
